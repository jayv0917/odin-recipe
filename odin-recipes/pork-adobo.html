Pork Adobo Recipe

Back to Home

Description

Pork adobo is a classic Filipino dish featuring pork braised in a flavorful mixture of vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and spices.
It's a savory and slightly sweet stew, with a balance of tangy and salty notes. The dish is known for its tender pork and rich, reduced sauce, often served with rice.

Ingredients To Use:

  • Pork belly
  • Garlic
  • Dried bay leaves
  • Vinegar
  • Soy sauce
  • Peppercorn
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Combine the pork belly, soy sauce, and garlic then marinade for at least 1 hour
  2. Heat the pot and put-in the marinated pork belly. Cook this all up for a few minutes
  3. Pour the remaining marinade including the garlic.
  4. Add water, whole peppercorn, and dried bay leaves. Then bring your mixture to a boil. Simmer for 40 minutes to 1 hour
  5. Put the vinegar inside and simmer for 12 to 15 minutes
  6. Add salt to taste
  7. Serve hot. Share and enjoy!
