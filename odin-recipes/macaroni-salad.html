Macaroni Salad Recipe

Back to Home

Description

Macaroni Salad is a common dish served at parties or events.

Filipino Sweet Macaroni Salad is a popular version of mac salad in the Philippines.

Some people associate this salad with the Christmas season.

Ingredients To Use:

  • 3/4 lb. elbow macaroni
  • 1 pack Lady's Choice Mayonnaise 220 ml
  • 1 can fruit cocktail 15 oz.
  • 1 can condensed milk 14 oz.
  • 3/4 cup raisins
  • 3/4 cup kaong
  • 3/4 cup nata de coco
  • 6 oz. cheddar cheese cubed
  • 2 quarts water

Steps:

  1. Cook the macaroni by boiling water in a pot. Add the macaroni. Stir and cook for 7 minutes. Drain the water and arrange macaroni in a large bowl.
  2. Add fruit cocktail, pineapple, kaong, nata de coco, cheese, and raisins. Gently mix all the ingredients.
  3. Pour condensed milk and then add Lady's Choice Mayonnaisse. Fold until all ingredients are well blended.Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
  4. Remove from the fridge and serve. Share and enjoy!
